## 🌟 **About This Series**

**Title:** Controlled Pulse  
**Season:** 1  
**Genre:** Medical Romance × Sci-Fi Thriller  
**Setting:** Hong Kong, 2026  

In the neon-drenched rain of a stormy night, Dr. Lin Muqing's ordinary life takes a dramatic turn. What begins as a routine rescue mission quickly spirals into a dangerous conspiracy that threatens to unravel everything she believes in.

## 📚 **Season 1 Chapters**

### **Chapter 1: Rescue in the Storm**
**Word Count:** ~936 words

*Perfect for readers who enjoy: medical drama, suspenseful beginnings, and strong female protagonists.*

*Perfect for readers who enjoy: medical authenticity, character development, and slow-burn romance.*
